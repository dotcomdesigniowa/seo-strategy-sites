Overview & Strategy Approach

This document outlines a comprehensive, data-driven SEO strategy designed specifically for Texas Building Center. The primary goal is to increase online visibility, drive qualified traffic, and generate consistent leads across the Texas Hill Country for barndominiums, custom homes, and metal buildings. This plan applies a Go Wide strategy: the keyword "barndominium" carries 246,000 monthly searches nationally, which is 30 times the volume of the next keyword family in the same service category. When a single keyword dominates at that scale, small businesses gain far more from building geographic presence across many markets for that one term than from spreading combinations across many lower-volume variants. As a result, the 5 selected keywords are distributed across 10 confirmed service area cities, with the barndominium anchor keyword receiving the most city slots. Every keyword was selected based on national search volume data, variant family grouping, commercial intent signals, and service-line alignment, with the result being a plan that maximizes the value of every dollar invested.

Market Analysis & Tiering

We analyzed all 29 cities and communities confirmed in Texas Building Center's service area and evaluated each market based on population size, proximity to the Burnet headquarters, and overall search opportunity. Because this plan applies a Go Wide strategy driven by the dominant barndominium keyword (246,000 monthly searches), all 10 of the highest-population service area cities are included in the current Level C plan. Tier 1 markets are the five highest-population communities: Marble Falls, Lampasas, Kingsland, Burnet (HQ), and Granite Shoals. Tier 2 markets are the next five confirmed service area cities: Horseshoe Bay, Llano, Cottonwood Shores, Johnson City, and Sunrise Beach. All 10 cities receive the barndominium keyword; secondary keywords are distributed across the top 7 to 8 cities based on strategic priority. Tier 3 markets are smaller Hill Country communities that become viable targets as domain authority grows over time.

This is the core deliverable of the Level C plan. The matrix below maps 5 high-value keywords across 10 confirmed service area cities for a total of 40 targeted keyword-city combinations. This is a Go Wide strategy: "barndominium" (246,000 monthly searches) anchors the plan with coverage in all 10 cities, while "barndominium plans" (60,500/mo) and "barndominium builders" (8,100/mo) cover the top 8 cities, and "custom home builders" (12,100/mo) and "metal buildings" (33,100/mo) cover the top 7 cities. Each combination represents a unique page designed to rank Texas Building Center for that specific search in that specific city. "Near me" variants are excluded from the matrix; the city name appended to each keyword in the URL slug and page title serves the same geographic function.
