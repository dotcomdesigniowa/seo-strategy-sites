Overview & Strategy Approach

This report outlines a data-driven local SEO strategy for MEK Interiors & Floors, a premier flooring contractor headquartered in Houston, Texas, serving the Greater Houston area since 1974. The goal of this strategy is to surgically place MEK Interiors & Floors at the top of search results for high-value, purchase-intent keywords in strategic, high-growth markets. By targeting search terms that signal a customer is ready to hire a contractor, this plan maximizes the value of every dollar invested in SEO.

The competitive landscape for flooring contractors in a major metropolitan area like Houston is intense. This strategy adopts a "Go Deep" approach: rather than scattering efforts across dozens of cities with low-impact keywords, we concentrate on a select group of high-volume, high-intent keywords within two primary markets. This ensures MEK Interiors & Floors builds authority and dominates search rankings for the most profitable service lines, including both residential and commercial flooring.
