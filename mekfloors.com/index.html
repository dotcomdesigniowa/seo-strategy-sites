Overview & Strategy Approach

This document outlines a comprehensive, data-driven SEO strategy designed specifically for MEK Interiors & Floors. The primary goal is to increase online visibility, drive qualified traffic, and generate consistent leads within the Greater Houston service area. The strategy is built on a foundation of high-intent, commercially relevant keywords mapped to the highest-value geographic market: Houston itself. Rather than spreading a small plan thin across multiple suburbs, this strategy concentrates all 10 keyword-city combinations in Houston, the dominant regional search hub with over 2.3 million residents and the highest local search volume in the region. Every keyword was selected based on national search volume data, commercial intent signals, and strategic service-line coverage, with deliberate inclusion of commercial flooring keywords to support MEK Interiors & Floors' growing commercial contract division.

Market Analysis & Tiering

We analyzed all major cities and communities within the Greater Houston service area and evaluated each market based on population size, proximity to MEK Interiors & Floors' Houston headquarters, and overall search opportunity. The analysis produced three tiers of market priority. Tier 1 markets represent the highest-population, highest-opportunity targets and form the backbone of the strategy. For the current Booster plan (10 combinations), concentrating all keyword-city combinations in Houston is the strongest possible approach: Houston's 2.3 million residents generate search volume that dwarfs any suburb, and a single well-targeted city outperforms a diluted split across two or more markets at this plan level. Tier 2 and Tier 3 markets are strong candidates for future plan expansion as domain authority grows.
